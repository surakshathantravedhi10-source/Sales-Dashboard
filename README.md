ShopNest Store — Power BI Sales Dashboard

A single-page Power BI dashboard analyzing ShopNest's e-commerce sales, with two views toggled via left-rail buttons: **Sales Overview** and **Operations and Customer Insights**.

View 1 — Sales Overview
![Sales Overview](sales-overview.png)

- Revenue KPI card
- Seasonal Sales Patterns (line chart, 2016–2018)
- Top 10 Product Categories by Sales (column chart)
- Top 5 / Lowest 5 States by Sales (column charts)
- Revenue by Year (column chart)
- Sales by State (map)

View 2 — Operations and Customer Insights
![Operations and Customer Insights](operations-customer-insights.png)

- On Time vs. Total Delays (combo chart, monthly trend)
- Payment Method Distribution (pie chart)
- Top / Bottom Products by Review Score (bar charts)
- Total Delays by Product Category (bar chart)
- Sales by State (map)

How to Use:
Use the navigation buttons to switch between views. Filter by Revenue, and use drillthrough on the delays chart for category-level detail.
